About MapMySpa

What is MapMySpa?

MapMySpa is a free, easy-to-use directory that helps people across the United States find medical spas near them. Whether you're looking for Botox, laser treatments, HydraFacials, or other aesthetic services, MapMySpa makes it simple to discover providers in your area.

Just enter your city name or ZIP code and instantly get a list of medical spas with their addresses, phone numbers, hours of operation, ratings, and real customer reviews.

Search by any US city or ZIP code and get results instantly.

Get addresses, phone numbers, and hours for every spa.

Ratings & Reviews

See real ratings and customer reviews before you book.

Hours & Status

View full weekly hours so you can plan your visit.

How it works

MapMySpa uses advanced AI technology to search for and compile information about medical spas across the United States. When you enter a location, our system retrieves relevant spa listings including contact details, operating hours, and customer feedback.

We are constantly working to improve our coverage and accuracy. If you notice a listing that is outdated or incorrect, we appreciate your patience as our database continues to grow.
