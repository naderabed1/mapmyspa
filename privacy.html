Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MapMySpa.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit and use our website.

Information we collect

When you use MapMySpa, we may collect the following:

  • Search queries: The city names or ZIP codes you enter when searching for medical spas.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with our site, including pages visited, time spent, and browser type.
  • Device information: General information about the device you use to access our site.

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number to use MapMySpa.

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve our medical spa search service.
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can make it better.
  • To monitor for technical issues and ensure the site functions properly.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Google AdSense and advertising

MapMySpa uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience and enable advertising. These include analytics cookies and advertising cookies used by Google AdSense. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some site functionality.

Third-party services

  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements.
  • Google Fonts — for loading website fonts.
  • Anthropic AI — to power our search results. Queries are processed through Anthropic's API.
  • Cloudflare — for security and performance.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data that cannot identify any individual.

Children's privacy

MapMySpa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will update the "Last updated" date when we do. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
